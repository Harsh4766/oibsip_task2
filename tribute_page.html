Narayana Murthy

Indian IT industrialist and co-founder of Infosys

Narayana Murthy (Born :- 1946)

Short biography of Narayana Murthy

  • Born on August 20, 1946, in Mysore, Karnataka, India.
  • Graduated with a degree in Electrical Engineering from the National Institute of Engineering, University of Mysore.
  • Worked at IIM Ahmedabad as the chief systems programmer.
  • Co-founded Infosys in 1981 with six other software professionals with an initial capital of INR 10,000.
  • Served as CEO of Infosys from 1981 to 2002 and as Chairman from 2002 to 2011.
  • Pioneered the Global Delivery Model, which revolutionized the outsourcing industry.
  • Known for his emphasis on values, ethics, and corporate governance.
  • Honored with the Padma Vibhushan (2008) and Padma Shri (2000) by the Government of India.
  • Active in philanthropic efforts through the Infosys Foundation.
  • Considered a pioneer in the Indian IT industry and a role model for aspiring entrepreneurs.
